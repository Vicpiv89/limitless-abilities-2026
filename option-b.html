About the Conference

Where Possibility Has No Limits

The international conference 'Endless Possibilities: Amputation. Rehabilitation. Social Adaptation.' returns in 2026 — this time in person, with online streaming. In 2025, the two-day online marathon attracted over 1,600 participants from across Ukraine and beyond.

This year we bring even more people together — on-site in Ivano-Frankivsk and online worldwide. Two days of masterclasses, seminars, solution showcases, and a job fair for people with disabilities. A catalyst for systemic change in prosthetics, orthotics, and rehabilitation.

Conference Format

What to Expect

Masterclasses

Hands-on sessions led by international experts in prosthetics, orthotics, and rehabilitation technology.

Seminars

Presentations on the latest clinical developments in amputation care, orthotics, and social adaptation.

Career Fair

Employment and opportunity connections for persons with disabilities — first of its kind in Ukraine.

Showcase

Live demonstrations from leading prosthetics and rehabilitation companies worldwide.
