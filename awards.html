About the Award

Recognizing Those Who Move the Field Forward

The Limitless Abilities Awards celebrate professionals, teams, and individuals who are transforming prosthetics, orthotics, and rehabilitation in Ukraine and internationally.

From clinical excellence to recovery journeys, from technical mastery to international collaboration — these awards acknowledge the full spectrum of contributions to the field.
