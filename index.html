Where Possibility Has No Limits

The international conference "Limitless Abilities: Amputation. Rehabilitation. Social Adaptation" returns in 2026 in a new format. After the success of the 2025 two-day online marathon, which brought together over 1,600 participants from Ukraine and other countries, the conference will take place offline for the first time.

Over two days, participants will join lectures, masterclasses, practical demonstrations, discussions, a solutions exhibition, and a job fair for people with disabilities. The event will bring together specialists, manufacturers, employers, the public sector, non-governmental organizations, and people with lived experience to shape the future of prosthetics, orthotics, and rehabilitation in Ukraine together.

All talks will be available to participants as recordings after the conference.

Limitless Abilities Awards 2026

Your work deserves recognition!

If you are changing the field of rehabilitation, prosthetics, or orthotics, introducing new approaches, building quality services, or inspiring others by your own example — apply for the Limitless Abilities Awards 2026.

The award covers 9 categories recognizing specialists, teams, organizations, and people making a significant contribution to the development of prosthetics, orthotics, rehabilitation, and accessibility — from students and clinicians to center directors, international partners, and people whose stories inspire others.

The winners will be announced during the Limitless Abilities Conference 2026.

Applications are open until August 9, 2026.
